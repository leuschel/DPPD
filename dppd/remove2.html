The "remove2" Benchmark

Part of the DPPD Library.

General Description

This is an even more sophisticated deforestation example, handed over to me by Jesper Jorgensen and adapted from Turchin's paper The concept of a supercompiler in TOPLAS, 8(3):292-325, 1986.

The benchmark program

rr(X,Y) :- f(X,T), f(T,Y).

f([],[]).
f([A|T],Y) :- h(A,T,Y).

h(A,[],[A]).
h(A,[B|S],Y) :- g(A,B,[B|S],S,Y).

g(A,A,_,S,[A|Y]) :- f(S,Y).
g(A,B,T,_,[A|Y]) :- A \== B,f(T,Y).

The partial deduction query

 :- rr(X,Y).

The run-time queries

 :- rr([a,a,b,b,a,a,a,a,c,d,a,b,a,a,d,d],Y).
 :- rr([a,b,c,d,e,f,g,h,i,j,k,l,m,n,o,p,q,r,s,t,u,v,w,x,y,z],Y).
 :- rr([a,b,b,b,c,d,e,f,g,g,h,i,j,k,l,m,m,m,m,m,m,m,m,
		n,o,p,q,r,s,t,u,v,v,v,v,w,x,y,z,z,z],Y).
